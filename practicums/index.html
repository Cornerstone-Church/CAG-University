Beyond the Classroom

We believe that education doesn't end in the classroom.
Practicums exist toprovide students with hands-on
leadership experience within the ministries of Cornerstone Church.

Pastoral Leadership

For students who have a unique calling to empower the local church through pastoral leadership. This practicum is hands-on in the areas of preaching, teaching, pastoral care, counseling, and missions.

Next Gen

Do you have a heart for the next generation? Join our team in leading ministry to the youth and children of Life Church and beyond.

Church Ministries

Administration, event organizing, and team building are vital components of any healthy organization. This practicum allows students to learn and lead the inner workings of Cornerstone Church

Creative Communication

If your gifts compel you towards service production, this practicum might be for you. Creative Communication includes everything from videography to leading worship and everything in between.
