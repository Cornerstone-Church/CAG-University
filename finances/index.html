Scholarship Opportunities

It is our desire that lack of finances never become a hindrance to attending Cornerstone School of Ministry. Other than the hundreds of scholarships available for high GPA and community involvement, we have scholarships of our own to hand out on an annual basis.

Federal Student Aid

The Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) continues to be the #1 source of grants and low interest loans for students. This is a quick and easy process that can be completed entirely online. You can begin filing for FAFSA during your senior year of high school starting on January 1st.
